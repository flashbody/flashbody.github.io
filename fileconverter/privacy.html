Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

FileMorph ("we", "our", or "the App") is a local file format converter for iOS. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

All file conversions are performed entirely on your device. No files are uploaded to our servers, and we do not have access to your files.

Local Processing

FileMorph uses only on-device processing powered by iOS native frameworks (PDFKit, UIKit). Your files never leave your device.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics SDKs, tracking tools, or third-party data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of unintended data collection.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
