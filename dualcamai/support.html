Frequently Asked Questions

Which iPhone models are supported?
DualCam AI requires iPhone 12 or newer running iOS 15+. The dual-camera simultaneous capture feature uses AVCaptureMultiCamSession, which requires Apple A12 Bionic chip or newer. On unsupported devices, the dual-camera entry point is automatically hidden.
How do I restore my Pro purchase on a new device?
Open DualCam AI → Settings → "Restore Purchase". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. Your lifetime license is tied to your Apple ID.
Why does the app get warm during long recordings?
Running two cameras simultaneously with AI processing is computationally intensive. DualCam AI uses iOS's dynamic power management to reduce AI inference frequency when thermal limits are approached, prioritizing frame rate stability over AI enhancement quality.
Can I use DualCam AI on iPad?
DualCam AI is currently optimized for iPhone only. iPad support is planned for a future update.
How do I get a refund?
All purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and follow Apple's refund process.
The live caption feature isn't working. What should I do?
Live captions require Speech Recognition permission. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Speech Recognition and enable it for DualCam AI. Also ensure your selected caption language is downloaded for on-device recognition in Settings → General → Language & Region.
Where are my saved videos and photos?
All captures are saved to your Photos app in the "DualCam AI" album. For dual-track recordings (Pro), all files are saved to the same album.
