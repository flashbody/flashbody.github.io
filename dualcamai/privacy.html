Privacy Policy

DualCam AI · Last updated: June 20, 2026

English 简体中文 日本語 Deutsch
Summary: DualCam AI processes all data 100% on-device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, photos, or videos to external servers. Privacy First

1. Information We Collect

DualCam AI is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect no personal information.

Data Processed On-Device Only

  • Camera data: Front and rear camera feeds are processed locally in real-time for dual-camera capture. No images or video are transmitted externally.
  • Microphone audio: Used solely for video recording and live captions (V3.0). Audio is processed on-device and never uploaded.
  • Photos & Videos: Saved directly to your device's photo library. We have no access to your library beyond what you explicitly share.
  • Face data: Used by Vision framework for face tracking and portrait segmentation. Processed entirely on-device, never stored or transmitted.
  • Speech recognition (V3.0): Uses Apple's on-device SFSpeechRecognizer. No audio leaves your device.

2. Purchase & Payment Data

All purchases are processed by Apple's App Store via StoreKit 2. DualCam AI never sees or stores your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all transaction data.

Purchase status (whether you have Pro access) is stored locally on your device via StoreKit's secure receipt validation.

3. Analytics & Crash Reporting

DualCam AI does not include any third-party analytics SDKs (e.g., Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude). We do not track usage patterns, session lengths, or feature usage.

If you opt into Apple's App Store crash reporting, Apple may share anonymized crash logs with us to improve stability. This is controlled entirely by your iOS privacy settings.

4. Permissions We Request

  • Camera — Required for dual-camera capture. Used only while the app is in the foreground.
  • Microphone — Required for video recording with audio and live captions. Used only during active recording.
  • Photo Library (Add Only) — To save your captures. We request add-only permission; we do not read your existing photos.
  • Speech Recognition (V3.0) — For live caption feature. On-device only, optional.

You can revoke any permission at any time in Settings → Privacy. Revoking camera access will disable the app's core functionality.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. DualCam AI has no backend servers, no cloud storage, and no advertising networks.

6. Children's Privacy

DualCam AI does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app contains no age-restricted content and is suitable for all ages.

7. Data Security

Since all processing is on-device, your data security is protected by iOS's built-in security model, including app sandboxing, encrypted storage, and Face ID / Touch ID device protection.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be communicated via App Store release notes. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

9. Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions about data processed by Apple (purchases, crash reports), please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.
