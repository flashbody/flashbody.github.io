Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the Live Activity not showing on my Lock Screen?

Open Settings → Notifications → Kickoff and enable Live Activities. Requires iOS 16.1 or later. On iPhone 14 Pro and later, the score will also appear in Dynamic Island.

My friend's room invite link doesn't open

Both of you must be signed into iCloud (in the same region recommended) and on iOS 16.0+. If the link still fails, ask your friend to re-create the room — invite codes expire after 24 hours.

How do I restore my Pro purchase on a new device?

Open Kickoff → Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Does Kickoff involve real-money betting or gambling?

No. The Prediction Plaza is for entertainment only. There are no prizes, no real money, and no gambling of any kind. It is purely a social leaderboard.

Which leagues are supported?

1,100+ leagues worldwide — Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A, Ligue 1, UEFA Champions League, FIFA World Cup, Chinese Super League, J.League, K League, MLS, and many more.

How do I cancel a subscription?

Kickoff Pro is a one-time purchase, not a subscription. There is nothing to cancel. If you purchased it by accident, please email us within 48 hours and we will help you get a refund through Apple.

Is my data private?

Yes. All room data is stored in your personal iCloud account via Apple CloudKit. We have no servers and we cannot read your data. There are no ads and no third-party tracking.
