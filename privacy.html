Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-07

Kickoff is built privacy-first. We collect as little as possible.

What we collect

  • Anonymous crash reports (Apple's CrashReporter, opt-in).
  • Anonymous usage diagnostics (active screens, latency). No personal IDs.
  • Your in-app preferences (favorite teams, language) — stored locally only.

What we do NOT collect

  • Your name, email, phone number.
  • Your location (precise or coarse).
  • Your contacts.
  • Any third-party advertising identifiers (we have no ads).

Bring Your Own Key (BYOK)

If you provide your own API key (API-Football, OpenAI, Claude, Gemini), it is stored encrypted in your iOS Keychain. We never see it.

World Cup Room

Room data (chat, emoji, betting selections) is stored on Supabase Realtime, encrypted in transit. Rooms are ephemeral and deleted within 30 days after the World Cup ends.

Children

Kickoff is rated 4+ but recommended for ages 13+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We will notify you in-app when this policy changes.

Contact

For privacy inquiries, please email flashbody@foxmail.com.

We respond within 7 business days.
