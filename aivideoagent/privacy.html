Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Short version: AIVideoAgent processes all audio entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or audio recordings.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

AIVideoAgent is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect:

  • Audio recordings or transcriptions
  • Personal identification information
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data

2. On-Device Processing

All AI features — speech recognition, speaker separation, translation, and voice synthesis — run entirely on your device using Apple's Neural Engine. No audio or text is ever sent to external servers.

3. Screen Recording Permission

AIVideoAgent requests screen recording permission solely to capture audio from other applications for real-time translation. This permission is used exclusively for audio capture. No video frames are recorded, stored, or transmitted.

4. Purchase Information

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. AIVideoAgent does not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or any purchase details beyond a local confirmation that a purchase has been made.

5. Network Access

AIVideoAgent may use network access during initial setup to download AI model files. After setup, the app functions entirely offline. No audio or personal data is transmitted over the network at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

AIVideoAgent does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not include features directed at children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
