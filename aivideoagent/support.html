Support

We're here to help. Find answers below or contact us directly.

How do I start using AIVideoAgent?

Open the app, tap "Start Listening," then play any video in another app or browser. AIVideoAgent will automatically capture the audio and begin translating in real time.

Why do I need to grant screen recording permission?

AIVideoAgent uses Apple's ScreenCaptureKit (Mac) or ReplayKit (iPhone) to capture audio from other apps. This is the only way to hear what's playing in a video. No video frames are ever recorded or stored.

Does it work with Netflix, YouTube, or other streaming services?

Yes. On Mac, AIVideoAgent captures system audio from any app or browser. On iPhone, you start a screen broadcast from Control Center and select AIVideoAgent. Note: DRM-protected content (Netflix, Disney+) may show a black screen on iPhone, but audio translation still works normally.

Does it require an internet connection?

No. All AI processing (speech recognition, speaker separation, translation, and voice synthesis) runs entirely on your device. After the initial model setup, AIVideoAgent works completely offline.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open AIVideoAgent → Settings → Restore Purchase. Your one-time purchase is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device signed in with the same account.

Which languages are supported?

Currently: English, Japanese, Korean, French, German, and Spanish → Chinese translation. More language pairs will be added in future free updates.

The translation seems delayed. How do I fix it?

On first launch, AI models need a few seconds to warm up. If delays persist, try closing other apps to free up memory, or reduce the number of active speakers in Settings.
